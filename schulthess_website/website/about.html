Every Fabric Carries a Story

We do not look at fabrics merely as items to be processed or washed. We look at them as beautiful containers of stories, deep physical textures, and priceless human memories. Schulthess makes washing an act of absolute preservation.

A Chronology of Excellence

Over 180 Years of Innovation

1845

Founding & Swiss Roots

Schulthess Group is established in Switzerland, beginning a legacy of mechanical engineering. From day one, the focus remains on quality leadership and robust Swiss build standards.

1951

Europe's First Automatic Washer

Schulthess designs and launches Europe's very first automatic household washing machine, revolutionizing laundry care and setting the standard for automated mechanical drum actions.

1975

The Eco-Efficiency Shift

Schulthess pivots to address global resource needs. At this time, a standard washing machine required 55 litres of water per kilogram of laundry. The race to minimize energy and water begins.

2026

Indian Market Launch

Partnering exclusively with Swisstech Innovation, Schulthess enters the Indian subcontinent, offering custom architectural residential options, commercial systems, and heavy industrial products backed by factory-certified SLAs.

Go Green. Go Clean.

Uncompromised Eco-Intelligence

Schulthess is committed to preserving the planet. Over the last four decades, our engineering teams have reduced water and electricity demands to the bare minimum while maximizing performance.

Today, Schulthess home and commercial appliances use up to 88% less water and 83% less electricity per kilogram of laundry compared to legacy systems.

The Art of Fabric Care

Through our precise profiClean program maps and engineering, we treat delicate materials as individual design challenges.

Pure Silk & Embroidery

Schulthess replaces high-risk chemical dry cleaning with cradle-soft aqueous cycles that fully preserve the delicate finish, colors, and textile elegance of couture silks.

Fine Linen Architecture

Managing water immersion curves to eliminate core creasing. Protects flax fibers from mechanical stress, cutting downstream finishing and pressing labor down to a fraction.

Delicate Pure Wool

Utilizes RKI-compliant thermal controls and carefully regulated drum oscillation. Deep-cleans and sanitizes delicate knits without inducing shrinkage or fiber pilling.

High-Thread Cotton

Deploys dynamic multi-phase deep cleaning to purge oils and organic soils deeply trapped in luxury sheets, while completely preserving breathability and fabric density.
