Our Core Professional Capabilities

Washing Systems

From compact starLine commercial washers (7–8 kg) to proLine (7–33 kg) and massive maxLine partition loaders (16–90 kg). Engineered with segment-specific programmes and tested for 30,000 cycles.

Drying & Heat-pump Technology

High-efficiency commercial dryers utilizing condensation, heat-pump (TW), or direct exhaust (TA) systems. Features smart moisture sensor profiles to prevent textile degradation and minimize energy consumption.

Rotary Ironers & Finishing

Professional flatwork ironers designed for rapid output and pristine results in hospitality, food service, and care homes. Delivers uniform heating pressure and variable speed configurations.
